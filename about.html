Meet Sam

Hometown: Georgetown, LA

Interests: Bass Fishing, duck hunting

Favorite Lure: Punching or flipping grass with a 1 oz weight and a craw

Favorite Lake: Toledo Bend Reservoir

Boat: 2013 Puma FTD

I am 30 years old and I recently got married in September to my beautiful wife. I have three wonderful stepchildren with her. We settled down on top of a hill overlooking an 18 acre pond in a little town in Grant Parish,La which has giving me an edge of being able to fish everyday, even if I'm not out fishing in a tournament. I was introduced to fishing at a very young age through my dad and stepdad. Our main fishing spot was Toledo Bend, which is the best lake in the country right now in my opinion, ever since then I was hooked. I received my first boat when I turned 16 and I have been fishing for bass both recreationally and competitively since then.
